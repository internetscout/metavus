Purge Sample Records

This will allow a system administrator to purge out all of the sample data records that are distributed with the software package. All sample records with [--SAMPLE RECORD--] in the description and all sample collections with [--SAMPLE COLLECTION--] in the description will be deleted from the database. Once you click the purge button, the resources will be removed. Classifications and controlled names will also be removed automatically, provided they have not been assigned to other resources prior to purging.
